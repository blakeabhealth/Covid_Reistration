• Capital Region Covid-19 Vaccine Pre-Registration

    Our community is working to deliver vaccine to our residents in a phased approach. The purpose of this form is to gather information from residents about their interest in receiving COVID-19 vaccination. We will ask some questions to determine when you are able to get the COVID-19 vaccine. The information entered on this form will be kept confidential and will only be used to connect you with where you can register to receive a COVID-19 vaccine in the future. We'll send you email and/or text messages to keep you informed along the way.
